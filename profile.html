Quick FAQs
How do I fund my wallet?
Go to Wallet → Fund → Choose amount → Pay via email. We confirm and credit you.
Subscription not activated yet?
Email ngs.spark.tv0@gmail.com with your User ID and payment proof. Activated within 1 hour.
How does subscription queue work?
If you have an active plan, you can queue a new plan. It auto-activates when your current plan expires.
How do I withdraw my balance?
Go to Wallet → Withdraw. Enter your bank details and amount. Admin processes within 24 hours.
Can I change my wallet currency?
Yes, contact support with your User ID. Currency changes require manual verification.
